# LLMs & Legal Aid: Understanding Legal Needs Exhibited Through User Queries

**Source**: [arXiv 2501.01711](https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.01711) · 2025-01-03

## Key takeaways

- Analyzes 3,847 queries from 1,252 users interacting with GPT-4 for legal queries
- 29.95% of queries contain factual information; 70.05% do not
- 64.93% seek legal information; 35.07% seek action advice
- 28.57% impose requirements to shape model's answer
- Zero-shot GPT-4o used for query classification

## Core claims

Unlike prior work focusing on LLM accuracy/hallucinations, this paper analyzes the user query dimension. Using GPT-4o for classification, the study reveals three key dimensions: (1) factual info provision, (2) info vs advice seeking, and (3) answer requirements imposition.

## Notable quotes
