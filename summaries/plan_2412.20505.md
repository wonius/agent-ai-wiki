# Planning, Living and Judging: A Multi-agent LLM-based Framework for Cyclical Urban Planning

**Source**: [arXiv 2412.20505](https://arxiv.org/abs/2412.20505) · 2024-12-29

## Key takeaways

- Proposes Cyclical Urban Planning (CUP) — closed-loop iterative plan generation, evaluation, refinement
- Three components: Planning (generate/refine plans), Living (ABM simulation), Judging (QOL evaluation)
- Experiments on Huilongguan community in Beijing
- Experience metric improves with cycles (65.17% → 69.03% after 3 iterations)

## Core claims

Traditional urban planning is static and one-time. This paper introduces **Cyclical Urban Planning (CUP)** with three phases:

1. **Planning**: LLM agents collaboratively generate and refine urban plans with both planner and resident participation

2. **Living**: LLM-based agent-based modeling (ABM) simulates residents' behaviors — mobility and social behaviors

3. **Judging**: Automatically evaluates Quality of Life (QOL) and provides feedback for next cycle

The framework handles the fundamental challenge of **how to effectively evaluate and improve urban plans** throughout iterative processes.
